TIPS & MOTIVATION

AI-generated health tips and daily motivation to keep you going.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Dehydration reduces performance by up to 20%.

😴

Prioritise Sleep

Muscles grow during rest, not during workouts. Aim for 7–9 hours of quality sleep every night.

📅

Be Consistent

3 workouts a week for 3 months beats 7 workouts a week for 2 weeks. Consistency wins.

🧘

Manage Stress

High cortisol from stress stores fat and breaks down muscle. Take breaks, breathe, recover.

🥩

Eat Enough Protein

Aim for 0.8–1g of protein per kg of body weight daily to support muscle repair and growth.

📈

Track Progress

Take weekly photos or log your workouts. Seeing progress is the best motivation to continue.
